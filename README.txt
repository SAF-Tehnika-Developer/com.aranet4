Features 

Read and display data about air quality in your environment with Aranet Home:

 - With Aranet4, track CO2, temperature, humidity, and atmospheric pressure.
 - With Aranet2, measure temperature and humidity levels.
 - Display graphs depicting air quality changes over time.
 - Create workflows to trigger actions when air quality is decreasing.
